A Love Built at the Right Time

Our Story

Marco Polo & Elyannah

01

Where It All Began

He was a city-born engineer—steady, thoughtful, and practical. She was a probinsyana—grounded, family-oriented, and quietly strong. Both navigating the demanding world of construction, neither expected that somewhere between site inspections, they would discover something far more meaningful than work.

Their story began in January 2023, on a construction project that unknowingly became the foundation of something greater. MP worked as QA/QC for the general contractor, while Yana was part of the client’s inspectorate team. Amid routine site inspections, it was the small moments—the casual chats before and after work—where a connection quietly began to grow. What started as simple conversation soon became the part of the day they both looked forward to most.

At that time, Yana was in the midst of closing a chapter with a persistent suitor, realizing it wasn’t the kind of love she was seeking for herself. MP, on the other hand, was simply waiting—open, but not searching too hard—for the right person to come along. They let their connection grow naturally, moving from colleagues to friends, and eventually, to something deeper.

"Love does not consist in gazing at each other, but in looking outward together in the same direction."
— Antoine de Saint-Exupéry
02

Choosing Love

Yana, who deeply valued her family, wanted her relationship to have their blessing. Love, for her, wasn’t just a feeling—it was a commitment that honored them as well. On April 30, 2023, MP traveled with her to her province to respectfully ask for her family’s blessing and experience the life she came from. It was a defining moment—one that transformed intention into sincerity.

When they returned to Manila, their hearts were full, yet shadowed by uncertainty. The joy of newfound love was tempered by the reality that MP would soon leave the country to pursue career opportunities abroad. Yana wrestled with doubt—was it wise to continue something that would be tested by distance so early on?

But love, when it is real, finds its way.

On the evening of May 7, 2023, under city lights at a rooftop restaurant, they chose each other—officially. Just three days later, on May 10, their relationship faced its first challenge as MP left the country, beginning their long-distance journey.

03

Love Across Distance

Distance and differences in time zone, however, did not weaken them—it strengthened their bond.

By June 7, MP returned to the Philippines, having gained clarity and realigned his priorities. His time away helped him see what mattered most, including the meaningful life he was building alongside Yana.

Their love deepened in presence and purpose. They grew together through shared values—anchored in faith, strengthened by the CFC-Singles for Christ community, and enriched by their shared hobbies and simple joys.

Their love wasn’t defined by grand gestures, but by steady devotion, mutual growth, and the daily choice to be there for each other.

04

From Foundations to Forever

On August 31, 2025, MP once again stood before Yana’s family—this time carrying a deeper promise in his heart. He asked for their blessing, not only to love Yana, but to spend his life with her.

On November 1, 2025—her birthday—MP knelt down and asked the question that had long been written in their story. She said yes.

He was her first. She would be his last.

Their love arrived not too early, not too late—but exactly when it was meant to. At a time when Yana had healed and was ready to open her heart, and when MP was ready to give his whole heart to someone truly deserving.

Their paths could have easily missed each other—if MP had left earlier, or if Yana hadn’t taken the leap to move to Manila. But fate, faith, and perfect timing brought them together.

Amid concrete structures and blueprints, they built something far more lasting: a love guided by faith and family, nurtured by daily choices, mutual understanding, and heartfelt appreciation for one another.
